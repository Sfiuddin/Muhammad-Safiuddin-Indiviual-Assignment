About Me

Go through this website to know more about myself and my gaming life. Don't forget to relate about myself with yours to make it more interesting! You can also use this website as a guidance for you to seek your inner talent in gaming. Video games play a prominent position in everyday life anywhere you look today, and regardless of whether it is from a gamer's point of view, a journalist's or a teacher's, video games evoke a very real opinion. These can range from being a good thing, a bad thing or even an evil thing, to video games. Video games can provide hours of entertainment for kids and adults alike when played for fun, for entertainment, for pleasure. It's about time that the newspapers, politicians and scaremongering folk begin to understand that, despite their 'warnings', video games are here to stay, and that the few do not equal the majority. Last but not least, there is a phrase 'Blame the person, not the medium'.
